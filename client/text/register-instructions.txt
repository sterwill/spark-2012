
  REGISTRATION INSTRUCTIONS

  Provide the name you want to be called and and e-mail address
  where you can be contacted.  This address will only be used
  to contact you during your employment and will never be shared
  with any other party.
  
  When you submit your information you will be assigned a unique
  ID by the system.  This ID is encoded in a badge that you are 
  requested to keep visible at all times.
  
  Your badge is your passport to all Tail Feather systems.
  
  Type 'cancel' to stop the registration process.
